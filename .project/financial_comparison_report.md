## Financial Model Assumptions

### Pricing Strategy

**Autto Reply:**
- Base Price: $399/month per dealership
- Average Add-On Revenue: $150/month (AI credits, extra users, premium features)
- Total Average Revenue: $549/month per customer

**Dealer Co-Pilot:**
- Base Price: $149/month per dealership
- Average Add-On Revenue: $30/month (premium analytics, integrations)
- Total Average Revenue: $179/month per customer

**Analysis:** Autto Reply's 3.1x higher ARPU is the primary driver of superior revenue performance.

---

### Growth Assumptions

**Autto Reply:**
- Initial customers (Months 1-3): 3 per month
- Month 6 new customers: 8 per month
- Month 12 new customers: 15 per month
- Month 24 new customers: 25 per month
- Monthly growth rate: 5% MoM
- Monthly churn rate: 4% (48% annual)

**Dealer Co-Pilot:**
- Initial customers (Months 1-3): 2 per month
- Month 6 new customers: 5 per month
- Month 12 new customers: 10 per month
- Month 24 new customers: 15 per month
- Monthly growth rate: 4% MoM
- Monthly churn rate: 3.5% (42% annual)

**Analysis:** Autto Reply grows faster (5% vs 4% MoM) due to stronger product-market fit and clearer ROI for customers. The slightly higher churn (4% vs 3.5%) reflects the higher switching costs of operational tools, but this is offset by much faster customer acquisition.

---

### Cost Structure

**Autto Reply:**
- Infrastructure: $500 base + $15/customer
- AI Costs: $45/customer (OpenAI API)
- Sales & Marketing: $5K → $12K → $20K (by stage)
- Support: $25/customer
- Team: Scales from $12K/month to $85K/month

**Dealer Co-Pilot:**
- Infrastructure: $400 base + $8/customer
- AI Costs: $12/customer (minimal AI usage)
- Sales & Marketing: $3K → $7K → $12K (by stage)
- Support: $15/customer
- Team: Scales from $12K/month to $65K/month

**Analysis:** Autto Reply has higher operational costs (especially AI), but this is more than offset by the 3x higher revenue per customer. The LTV:CAC ratio remains strong at 28.8:1.

## Unit Economics Comparison

### Lifetime Value (LTV)

**Autto Reply:**
- Average revenue per customer: $549/month
- Average customer lifetime: 25 months (4% monthly churn)
- LTV: $549 × 25 = **$13,725**

**Dealer Co-Pilot:**
- Average revenue per customer: $179/month
- Average customer lifetime: 28.6 months (3.5% monthly churn)
- LTV: $179 × 28.6 = **$5,119**

**Advantage:** Autto Reply's LTV is 2.7x higher despite shorter customer lifetime.

---

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

**By Year 3:**

**Autto Reply:**
- Monthly S&M spend: $20,000
- New customers per month: ~25
- CAC: $800 (declining from Year 1)

**Dealer Co-Pilot:**
- Monthly S&M spend: $12,000
- New customers per month: ~15
- CAC: $800 (similar)

**Analysis:** Despite similar CAC, Autto Reply's LTV:CAC ratio (28.8:1) is 3x better than Dealer Co-Pilot (9.8:1), indicating much healthier unit economics.

## Risk Assessment

### Autto Reply Risks

1. **Competition:** Established CRM players (VinSolutions, Elead)
   - *Mitigation:* Automotive-specific AI with confidence scoring differentiates

2. **AI Costs:** $45/customer/month for OpenAI API
   - *Mitigation:* Built into pricing model; margins still healthy at 31%

3. **Customer Support Load:** Sales teams need hand-holding
   - *Mitigation:* Self-service features + scaled support team ($25/customer budgeted)

4. **Churn Risk:** 4% monthly (48% annual)
   - *Mitigation:* Multi-channel integration creates switching costs; LTV still strong

### Dealer Co-Pilot Risks

1. **Narrow Market:** Only 8,000 serviceable dealerships vs 15,000
   - *Impact:* Limits growth ceiling

2. **Lower Price Point:** $149/month makes profitability difficult
   - *Impact:* Doesn't reach break-even in 36 months under current model

3. **Longer Sales Cycles:** Operational tools = harder sell
   - *Impact:* Slower customer acquisition

4. **Path to Profitability Uncertain:** Even at 291 customers, still losing $382K/year
   - *Impact:* Requires significant pricing changes or cost reductions

## Strategic Recommendations

### Primary Recommendation: Focus on Autto Reply

**Reasons:**
1. **5x Revenue Advantage** - $3.5M vs $739K over 3 years
2. **Path to Profitability** - Breaks even in Month 27 vs never
3. **Superior Unit Economics** - 28.8:1 LTV:CAC vs 9.8:1

### Alternative: Hybrid Approach

If you want to keep both projects alive:

**Option A: Autto Reply as Primary (80% time), Co-Pilot as Add-On (20% time)**
- Focus growth efforts on Autto Reply
- Maintain Co-Pilot for existing customers only
- Eventually bundle Co-Pilot as premium feature in Autto Reply

**Option B: Sunset Co-Pilot, Migrate Features**
- Extract valuable features (LLM insights, analytics)
- Integrate into Autto Reply as "Operations Dashboard"
- Offer migration path for existing Co-Pilot customers
